Process the incoming audio.

  • in: the input buffer from one of the ports
  • out: the output buffer to one of the ports
Note
Ports represent physical inputs and outputs of a the system's soundcard. As of release v1.0, only two ports are used since most systems have at least 2 (i.e. microphone in and LEFT channel of line out for a laptop)
